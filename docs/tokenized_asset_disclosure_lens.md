## Reviewer Questions

- Does the filing disclose who controls custody?
- Does the filing explain valuation source and fair value hierarchy?
- Does the filing distinguish onchain record from legal ownership?
- Does the filing describe impairment, loss, freeze, hack, or recovery events?
- Does the filing disclose material rights such as redemption, interest, dividend, or delivery?
